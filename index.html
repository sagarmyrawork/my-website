Understanding Hair Types

Straight Hair

Naturally smooth and shiny, straight hair tends to reflect more light and often becomes oily faster due to easy oil movement from scalp to ends.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair offers natural texture and volume. It benefits from hydration and lightweight products that enhance natural movement.

Curly Hair

Curly hair requires moisture retention and gentle care. Curl-friendly routines help reduce frizz and improve definition.

Coily Hair

Coily hair is beautifully textured and delicate. Deep conditioning and protective styling are especially important.

Essential Hair Care Tips

✔ Use a mild shampoo suitable for your scalp type

✔ Avoid excessive heat styling and harsh treatments

✔ Trim hair regularly to reduce split ends

✔ Maintain a protein-rich balanced diet

✔ Stay hydrated for stronger hair growth

✔ Massage your scalp to improve blood circulation

✔ Use wide-tooth combs to prevent breakage

✔ Protect hair from sun and pollution exposure

Healthy Hair Routines

Daily Routine

Gentle detangling, hydration, scalp cleanliness, and avoiding unnecessary heat exposure.

Weekly Routine

Hair oiling, deep conditioning, scalp massage, and nourishing treatment masks.

Monthly Routine

Trimming split ends, reviewing product effectiveness, and adjusting care methods based on seasonal needs.
