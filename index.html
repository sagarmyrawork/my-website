About Hair Health

Hair health depends on nutrition, lifestyle, genetics, and care routine. Small changes in daily habits can significantly improve hair strength and shine.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use mild shampoo 2–3 times per week
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Maintain protein-rich diet
  • Stay hydrated
